Alternative Accommodation

While we would love for everyone to stay with us at the venue, we know some guests may prefer alternative options. There is plenty of accommodation nearby to suit a range of budgets, all within a short drive of Ballymagarvey Village.

Please note that Ballymagarvey Village is in a rural location, and there is no organised transport to off-site accommodation.

Getting back to your accommodation:
  • We strongly recommend pre-booking taxis in advance, particularly for late-night collections.
  • The venue can provide local taxi numbers — just ask at reception on the day.
  • If possible, consider sharing taxis with other guests staying nearby.
